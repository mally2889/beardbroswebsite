The Agency

Two brothers. One obsession.

Beard Bros started as a bet that the right blend of creativity and technology could change how businesses connect — and became a Mumbai-based partner to 100+ brands across India and the US.

Where
we started a decade ago

Beard Bros was founded over ten years ago by two brothers who saw the digital world for what it could become, not just what it was. The bet was simple, and at the time unfashionable: that creativity and technology weren't separate disciplines — that the brands who fused them first would be the ones people actually remembered. A decade on, that bet has become a practice: 100+ brands partnered, ₹5 crore+ in media spend managed, and a specialism in building D2C brands that don't just launch — they compound.

What
we believe partners, not vendors

We don't take briefs — we take stakes. Every engagement runs on the same principle: strategy, story and craft aren't line items to be siloed across departments, they're one system, built by people who stay in the account long enough to be judged on the outcome, not the deck. That's the difference between a vendor and a partner, and it's the only relationship we're interested in building.
